Their Legendary Journey

Rohit Sharma

2007: International Debut

Made his debut against Ireland and impressed with his elegant strokeplay.

2013: First Double Century

Scored 209 vs Australia, becoming only the 3rd Indian to score an ODI double century.

2019: WC Record

Scored 5 centuries in a single World Cup, a record that still stands.

2023: WC Captaincy

Led India to the final with his inspirational leadership.

Virat Kohli

2008: U19 WC Win

Captained India to U19 World Cup victory, announcing his arrival.

2011: WC Winner

Played crucial knocks in India's triumphant World Cup campaign.

2018: Fastest to 10,000 ODI Runs

Broke Sachin Tendulkar's record for fastest to 10,000 ODI runs.

2023: Master of Chases

Led with composure and delivered numerous match-winning innings.

Ravindra Jadeja

2009: International Debut

Debuted in ODIs against Sri Lanka, showing early promise as an all-rounder.

2013: Champions Trophy Hero

Finished as the highest wicket-taker and played a key role in India’s title win.

2019: WC Knockout Knock

Almost pulled off a miracle in the semi-final with a fiery 77 and sharp fielding.

2023: Rock in the Middle

Provided balance with crucial spells and match-saving cameos throughout the tournament.
