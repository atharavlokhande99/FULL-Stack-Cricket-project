BATTING SKILLS AND MOMENTS

Deccan Chargers Beginnings (2008-2010)

Rohit started as a fearless middle-order talent, showing timing and range that hinted at a special IPL career.

Move to Mumbai Indians (2011)

The move unlocked consistency: smarter tempo control, better shot selection, and impact in pressure games.

Powerplay Dominance

When opening, Rohit attacks gaps and short balls early, giving Mumbai fast starts and scoreboard control.

The Pull Shot Trademark

His pull shot against pace is one of IPL's cleanest scoring options and a key reason for his big innings.

Big Match Temperament

Rohit often raises his level in playoffs and crunch league games with calm decision-making and clean striking.

Longevity and Evolution

From young hitter to complete batter, he adapted role and pace while staying one of IPL's most watched players.

IPL Captaincy

FIVE IPL TROPHIES - CAPTAINCY MOMENTS

2013 - First Crown

Rohit's first IPL trophy as MI captain, built on brave bowling calls in pressure overs.

2015 - Second Title

A complete campaign and composed final execution delivered MI's second title under Rohit.

2017 - Thriller Finish

In one of IPL's closest finals, Rohit's calm strategy helped MI seal a famous win.

2019 - Nerve Hold

Another tight final, another tactical masterclass as MI lifted trophy number four.

2020 - Five-Star Legacy

In the UAE season, MI looked unstoppable and Rohit completed his five-title captaincy legacy.
