About Ajinkya Rahane

Calm by nature, sharp in decision-making, and graceful at the crease.

Ajinkya Rahane has been one of India's most dependable Test batters, known for his compact technique and elegant strokeplay.

He earned immense respect for his calm leadership, especially during challenging overseas tours where temperament mattered as much as talent.

Rahane's contribution to Indian cricket goes beyond runs. He brought stability, humility, and a team-first attitude in pressure moments.

Career Highlights

Milestones that define Rahane's journey in Indian cricket.

Test Debut for India

Debuted in Test cricket and quickly became a trusted top-order option in demanding conditions.

India Test Vice-Captain

Served as vice-captain and became a key tactical figure in India's red-ball setup.

Border-Gavaskar 2020-21

Led India to a historic Test series win in Australia, one of the greatest overseas triumphs.

Overseas Centuries

Produced memorable hundreds abroad with composure and technical excellence.
